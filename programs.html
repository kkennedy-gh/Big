The Wellness Wheel

The Wellness Wheel defines eight categories that the typical human experiences in their lives. The comprehensive approach of this model accounts for the needs of a human being in our complex modern society.

 

We believe that focusing on each of these eight categories can be a powerful tool in improving one's life situation.

Research has shown that what we focus our thoughts on is a determining factor in how we feel emotionally.

 

Purposefully recalling positive experiences and focusing on them has shown to improve over-all mental well-being.

 

Showing kindness to others has the mutual benefit of increasing positive emotions in the person performing the act of kindness and also the person receiving it.
